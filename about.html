About Me

I am Lisa Reichardt, a senior UX/UI designer with 9+ years of experience. I am passionate about shaping digital products with an industrial design background. Currently I’m open to work.

Design does not happen by chance, but is the result of precise observation, deep understanding, and critical questioning. As an industrial and interface designer, I design with attitude, passion, and curiosity for people and context.

I particularly enjoy working at the interface between digital technology and physical products with the aim of creating experiences that are relevant, add value, and are future-proof. For me, trends and new technologies are not gimmicks, but tools for shaping real progress.

Complexity motivates me. To this end, I acquire in-depth domain knowledge and use it to develop sustainable solutions. The best ideas emerge in a team through a change of perspective, exchange, and collaborative thinking.

In my free time, I like to hop on my road bike and explore new areas and spending time with my family.
